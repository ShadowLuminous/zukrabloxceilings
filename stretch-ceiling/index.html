STRETCH CEILING SERVICES

RESIDENTIAL & COMMERCIAL STRETCH CEILING

CR Ceiling provides different stretch ceiling solutions to improve interior design systems and to provide conceptually new finishing product for a variety of applications within existing and developing projects. Our service is fast and exceptional, and we provide meticulous attention to detail, high-quality materials, exquisite taste, and a skilled professional installation team.

A stretch ceiling is a suspended or drop-ceiling system made up of two main elements: the aluminum frame and the durable PVC or canvas membrane. Installation is quick and easy and begins by attaching the frame to the wall or ceiling, followed by stretching the membrane taut over it.  No furniture removal is necessary; our team only takes up a few hours of your time and we never leave a mess. There are no limits on what you can do with a stretch ceiling. Whether you are applying it to your ceiling or a wall, you can completely change the look and feel of a room without doubling-down on a costly and messy renovation. You can hide existing popcorn ceilings and change your colour scheme, but you can get even more creative than that.
